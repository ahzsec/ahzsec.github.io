While protecting the decentralized economy is protecting the pioneering blockchain industry, and protecting the open economy is protecting the decentralized economy, The missions aim diverge and is observed through different points, Such that protecting the open economy is democratizing observation where whole systems and entities tends to participate due to the clear open routes and agreed upon policies, And while protecting the decentralized economy is protecting a form or a route of the open economy, The decentralized economy special features and distinct nature creates special problems, Where distinguishing the special route of the open economy wouldn’t situate nor rebound the critical issues, By redefining the decentralized economy as connective route that rebase the open economy and respire the inefficient routes we will create a world where the decentralized economy saturate and cultivate the incompetent entities, Not only freeing those entities but circulating more fair value of the open economy process, And the blockchain industry is pioneering by providing high-quality cut-edge self-assurance technologies that are rampantly growing as the core of the world we’re presenting, unique or lacking distinction, Protecting this technology is the principle.

— Ahmed “AHZ” Sinada on protecting decentralization, the open economy, and blockchain technology.
